FAQ

Questions we get daily.

Straight answers. No corporate speak, no confusion.

How long does it take to build a website?

A standard website or landing page takes 3–7 days. Complex projects like e-commerce stores or web apps take 2–4 weeks. We give you a clear timeline before we start — no surprises.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes — we require 50% upfront to begin work. The remaining 50% is paid on final delivery. This keeps both sides committed and protects your investment.

Can I see examples of your past work?

Absolutely. Check the Work section on this page for live examples. You can also DM us on WhatsApp and we'll send a full portfolio with more projects and case studies.

What if I don't like the design?

We include 2–3 revision rounds in every project. We work closely with you on the design before moving to development, and keep refining until you're happy.

Do you build for both mobile and desktop?

Always. Every website and app we deliver is fully responsive — looking and working perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers. Mobile-first is our standard.

How do I get started?
